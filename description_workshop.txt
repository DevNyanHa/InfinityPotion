[h2]Infinity Potion [BETA][/h2]
[url=https://discord.gg/XbxTxdf7jh][img]https://img.shields.io/badge/Discord-black?logo=discord[/img][/url] [url=https://github.com/DevNyanHa/InfinityPotion][img]https://img.shields.io/badge/Github-DevNyanHa-white?logo=github[/img][/url] [img]https://img.shields.io/github/v/release/DevNyanHa/InfinityPotion[/img] [img]https://img.shields.io/github/license/DevNyanHa/InfinityPotion[/img]
This mod makes all [b]consumable items[/b]-such as potions, boss summoning items, and ammo-[b]unlimited[/b].
If you want to [b]change the mod settings[/b], you can [b]easily[/b] do so at [u]Settings->Mod Settings->InfinityPotion->Main Config[/u].

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] You can use it without any crafting hassle-as long as you have just one consumable item.
[*] All consumable items, including potions, summoning items, and ammo, can be used [b]infinitely[/b].
[*] You can [b]customize[/b] which items are affected in the settings.
[*] Items with the [b][Infinity][/b] tag in their description can be easily [b]identified[/b].
[/list]
